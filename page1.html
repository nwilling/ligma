What is Valve?

Valve Corporation is a game development, game publishing, and game distribution company.They have made several revolutionary games, and some say, some of the best games to ever exist. An example of this is Portal. Portal first released on October 10, 2007. It is a great example because it shows how original the company was when it game togame development. Also, they own a digital distribution platform called Steam. Steam has been the go-to for PC gamers since it's release. It makes the process of buying, downloading, and playing your game's easy. It even has it's own community market where you can buy, trade, or sell in game items. Nowadays, they aren't developing games. But, they are in the market of virtual reality and have worked with HTC to make their own headset.
